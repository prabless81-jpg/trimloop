How it works

Set it once. Fill your chairs.

TrimLoop handles client retention so you can focus on the cut. Three steps, zero daily effort.

01

Connect your shop

Sign up and add your clients' phone numbers. Import from a spreadsheet or add them as you go.

02

Log each visit

After a cut, mark the client as visited. TrimLoop starts a 21-day countdown automatically.

03

SMS goes out

On day 21, your client gets a friendly text reminder to book their next appointment with you.
